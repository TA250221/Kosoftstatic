The Iliad Team

Boas Gonen, MD

Dr. Gonen is board certified in Internal Medicine and Endocrinology/Metabolism and has 11 years experience in academic medicine. His last academic position was Associate Professor of Medicine and acting head of the Endocrine division at Thomas Jefferson Medical College in Philadelphia. He was involved in seminal studies that established HbA1C as a test to diagnose diabetes and monitor the level of diabetic control. Dr. Gonen has more than 20 years of experience in the Pharmaceutical Industry, including senior R&D positions at Wyeth for 10 years and Teva Pharmaceuticals for 2 years. He has directed all stages of clinical development, primarily in the areas of Diabetes, Obesity, Bone disease, Neurology and Cardiovascular/Lipid disorders. He has sat on advisory boards for numerous companies and consulted on issues related to drug development, regulatory plans and business development. Most recently, Dr. Gonen was the CEO of Sella Pharmaceuticals which had a diagnostic product for Growth-Hormone Deficiency in advanced stages of clinical development.

The Iliad Team

Steven J. Adelman, PhD, FAHA

Dr. Steven Adelman is a senior pharmaceutical executive with more than 20 years experience in Drug Discovery and Therapeutic Intervention in Cardiovascular / Metabolic and Immune Disorders. Currently, he serves as President and CEO of VascularStrategies, a company focused on assessment and development of therapeutic agents utilizing novel and atypical approaches to Cardiovascular, Inflammatory and Metabolic Diseases. Dr. Adelman has practiced his expertise as Senior Director Discovery Research at Wyeth Pharmaceuticals where he played a major leadership role in bringing more than 10 pharmacologic agents from discovery research into clinical trials, including breakthrough programs in vascular biology and its relationship to atherosclerosis. Dr. Adelman was also a key initiator and driver of the first Drug Eluting Stent, the Cypher-stent, a product that has changed the paradigm for the practice of Interventional Cardiology. In addition, Dr Adelman has authored numerous articles in peer reviewed journals and presented extensively at numerous scientific symposiums. He is a recognized inventor, with more than 12 patents issued both in the U.S. and internationally. Dr. Adelman is also an elected fellow of the American Heart Association and serves as member and Chair on the review committees of several diabetes and cardiovascular societies.

Steve Tsetsekos

Mr. Tsetsekos has more than 20 years of experience in finance, private equity, venture capital and entrepreneurship. A serial entrepreneur, Mr. Tsetsekos has co-founded several biotechnology companies, including Nutrissea Holdings Inc., a company dedicated to the research and development of diabetes related therapeutics, and Propepgen Biostrategies Inc, a specialty pharmaceutical company pioneering research and drug development in the area of protein phosphatase activators and inhibitors. Past engagements for Mr. Tsetsekos have included Chief Financial Officer of the iBoom Network, Chief Operating Officer of Internet Entertainment Services Inc., and Vice President of Commercial Banking at M&T Bank and HSBC Bank. Currently, Mr. Tsetsekos serves on the Board of Directors of Iliad Ventures, Propepgen Biostrategies Inc., D&M Property Inc., and Nu-Gaming Ventures Inc. Mr. Tsetsekos is a graduate of Amherst College (BA Political Science, Cum Laude).

Poluru L. Reddy, Ph.D. DABCC


Dr. Reddy serves as the Medical Lab Director for Iliad Neurosciences Inc. where he manages all day to day operations of the laboratory. Board Certified in Molecular Diagnostics by American Board of Clinical Chemistry, Poluru Reddy has extensive experience in Cloning and SNP/CGH Arrays, Pharmacogenetics, Next Generation DNA Sequencing, Signal Transduction, Clinical Chemistry Assays, Receptor Function Analysis, Molecular Diagnostic Assays, and Mutation Detection Analysis. Dr. Reddy has supervised numerous Clinical Molecular Diagnostic laboratories for the past ten years. As such, he has developed and validated several molecular diagnostic assays for Oncology, Infectious diseases and inherited diseases as well as Molecular diagnostic assays for the detection of mutations in alpha and beta globin genes (Patented). A well renowned leader in his field, Dr. Reddy has inspected both National and International Clinical Laboratories as both a CAP inspector & team leader. Dr. Reddy has authored or co-authored over 45 scientific publications in the areas of molecular diagnostics and signal transduction. As a dedicated researcher in the sciences, Dr. Reddy has discovered the role of nitric oxide in kappa opioid tolerance and dependence process

About Us

Iliad Neurosciences Inc. is a dynamic life sciences company dedicated to the development of products for the diagnosis and treatment ("theranostic") of neuro-development disorders with emphasis on Autism and related conditions. Currently, Iliad has identified and licensed a late stage patented diagnostic technology related to Folate Receptor A Autoantibodies (FRA-Ab).
